Information

The recycling workshop is a place where people can come and work on recycling projects. The atelier is a room where people can work on art projects using recycled materials. The pottery room is a place where people can work on making pottery out of recycled materials. The woodworking shop is a place where people can work on making furniture out of recycled materials. The blacksmith room is a place where people can work on making metal objects out of recycled materials. Anyone can come and work in the workshop by paying a certain amount of money for the entrance and the materials they use. A person can hand over materials they don't need to the workshop, which will be processed and used.
